About House Price Prediction

Real-estate house pricing in current scenarios is a major headache when it comes
to new or even old buyers. Based on several items like – the area where the house
is, decoration, construction etc. the price varies significantly. Therefore when
a customer’s budget and expectation matches with the price then a deal takes place
quickly. And often times it is really hard to predict the price considering all these
ever-changing features.
